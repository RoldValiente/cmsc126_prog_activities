Philippine Income Tax Calculator

Made by Harold Clyde Valiente

This program computes the annual income tax payable of earning Filipino individual and business organizations.
It asks for your monthly income, number of dependents, and the type of worker you are (government or private).
After entering the necessary information correctly, it will calculate and display your annual income tax payable.
